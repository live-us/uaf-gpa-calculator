How to Calculate UAF CGPA?

At UAF, the Cumulative Grade Point Average (CGPA) is determined by calculating the ratio between the total quality points earned and the total credit hours attempted. Our calculator simplifies this complex mathematics:

  1. Enter Course Details: Add each course's credit hours and the grade you scored in the semester.
  2. Real-time Processing: The tool instantly multiplies your grade points with the credit hours to calculate quality points.
  3. Track Progress: Add multiple semesters to seamlessly blend your total credits and quality points to fetch your Cumulative CGPA.

SGPA vs CGPA at UAF: What's the Difference?

During your degree at the University of Agriculture, you will encounter two primary grading metrics:

  • SGPA (Semester Grade Point Average): Your performance in a single specific semester. It determines if you are placed on academic probation for that term.
  • CGPA (Cumulative Grade Point Average): The combined average of all completed semesters. This is the metric used for your final degree distinction, merit scholarships, and job placements upon graduation.
